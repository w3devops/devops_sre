An 8-week journey to master cloud-native technologies and SRE principles

Welcome to My Learning Journey

This repository documents my comprehensive 8-week journey to master DevOps and SRE skills, preparing for a role involving Kubernetes, GitOps, observability, CI/CD, and cloud technologies. Each week focuses on specific technologies with hands-on projects, configurations, scripts, and troubleshooting logs.

Week 1: Kubernetes & Docker Foundation

Complete Kubernetes mastery from basics to production operations.

  • Day 1: Kubernetes Basics - Architecture, pods, services
  • Day 2: Deployments & Configuration - ConfigMaps, Secrets, scaling
  • Day 3: StatefulSets & Storage - Persistent volumes, Ingress
  • Day 4: Security & Monitoring - RBAC, Prometheus, Helm
  • Day 5: CI/CD & GitOps - GitHub Actions, ArgoCD, advanced deployments
  • Day 6: Multi-Cluster & Operations - Backup, optimization, production ops

Week 2: Kubernetes Operators & Advanced Patterns

Master operators, controllers, and advanced Kubernetes development patterns.

  • Built custom controllers with controller-runtime
  • Created Kubernetes operators for complex applications
  • Designed CRDs and reconciliation patterns
  • Implemented admission controllers and webhooks

Week 3: Advanced Networking & Service Mesh

Master service mesh, advanced networking, and microservices communication.

  • Deployed Istio service mesh with mTLS
  • Implemented circuit breakers and traffic policies
  • Advanced network policies and CNI plugins
  • Service mesh observability and security

Week 4: Cloud Platforms & Infrastructure

Learn cloud-native Kubernetes and Infrastructure as Code.

  • Deployed and managed EKS/GKE clusters
  • Used Terraform for Kubernetes infrastructure
  • Integrated cloud services (databases, storage)
  • Multi-cloud and hybrid cloud strategies

Week 5: Advanced Observability & SRE

Master distributed tracing, logging, and SRE principles.

  • Implemented distributed tracing with Jaeger
  • Set up centralized logging with Loki
  • Defined SLOs/SLIs and error budgets
  • Built incident response and runbooks

Week 6: Production Operations & Troubleshooting

Master production operations, debugging, and performance optimization.

  • Advanced Kubernetes troubleshooting techniques
  • Performance analysis and optimization
  • Security hardening and compliance
  • Backup/restore strategies and testing

Week 7: Enterprise Patterns & Architecture

Learn enterprise-grade patterns, policy management, and cost optimization.

  • Multi-cluster management and federation
  • Policy as Code with Kyverno/OPA
  • GitOps security and advanced patterns
  • Cost optimization and FinOps practices

Week 8: Capstone & Interview Preparation

System design, chaos engineering, and comprehensive interview preparation.

  • Designed complex Kubernetes systems
  • Chaos engineering with LitmusChaos
  • Production case studies and war stories
  • Technical and behavioral interview preparation
